# Business Insights 360 | Power BI Dashboard for AtliQ Hardware

An interactive Power BI dashboard for AtliQ Hardware covering Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Executive views with KPI tracking, performance analysis, and forecast insights.

---

> Note: Due to GitHub file size limitations, the original `.pbix` file is not uploaded. The live dashboard and project documentation are provided instead.

---

## Overview

Business Insights 360 is a centralized Power BI dashboard designed to analyze AtliQ Hardware’s business performance across multiple functions. The report includes Finance, Sales, Marketing, Supply Chain, and Executive views to support better business decisions through data storytelling and KPI monitoring.

---

## Key Highlights

- Tracked **Net Sales of $19.37bn**
- Achieved **Gross Margin of 36.41%**
- Captured **Net Profit % of -20.71%**
- Monitored **Forecast Accuracy of 91.41%**
- Built detailed views for customer, product, regional, and sub-zone performance
- Included trend analysis across years, quarters, and YTD reporting

---

## Dashboard Views

- **Finance View** – Profit and loss analysis, net sales trends, benchmark comparison
- **Sales View** – Customer performance, product performance, unit economics
- **Marketing View** – Regional and product-wise profitability analysis
- **Supply Chain View** – Forecast accuracy, net error, absolute error, risk tracking
- **Executive View** – High-level KPIs, market share, revenue by division/channel, top customers and products

## Business Problem

Businesses often struggle to monitor performance across departments using disconnected reports. This project solves that by creating a unified dashboard that brings together finance, sales, marketing, supply chain, and executive-level insights in one place.

- Forecast accuracy and risk monitoring
- Decision-ready executive summary
